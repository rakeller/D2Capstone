About Us

This is what we do.

We are creating a way to combine cycling and music to allow users to have a productive yet fun workout experience. This concept has not been explored much, and we plan to change that. With help of some sensors, our algorithm will be able to track a cyclist's rotation speed and match that with a song's BPM. With music to go with your pace, it'll be a better experience for bikers.
